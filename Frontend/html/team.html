Meet Our Team

At MeetMyHarvest, our success is driven by the dedicated individuals who make up our team. Together, we share a common passion for transforming the agricultural landscape and fostering a seamless connection between farmers and buyers. Our diverse team comprises experts from various fields, all working collaboratively to achieve our mission. Here's a glimpse of the remarkable individuals who drive MeetMyHarvest's vision:
John Tighil
Founder & CEO
John Tighil is the visionary behind MeetMyHarvest, a seasoned entrepreneur with a deep-rooted passion for agriculture. He founded the platform with the aim of creating a space where farmers can thrive and buyers can access top-quality produce conveniently.
Fortune Ifeanyi Daberechi
CO.Founder & Head of Operations
Fortune Ifeanyi Daberechi oversees the day-to-day operations at MeetMyHarvest. His meticulous approach and knack for organization ensure that our platform runs smoothly, connecting farmers and buyers seamlessly.
David Johnson
Tech Guru
David Johnson is our technology whiz. He's responsible for ensuring that our platform stays cutting-edge, providing users with a seamless and efficient experience. His expertise in technology and innovation drives MeetMyHarvest's growth.
Robert Martinez
Marketing Maven
Robert Martinez is our marketing genius. His creative strategies and campaigns are responsible for spreading the word about MeetMyHarvest and connecting us with even more users.
At MeetMyHarvest, our team is more than just a group of individuals; we're a close-knit family with a shared vision of promoting ethical, sustainable, and transparent agriculture. We're committed to creating a brighter future for the agricultural industry and the communities we serve.
Join us on this journey toward a more connected and sustainable agricultural world. Together, we're sowing the seeds of change and reaping the harvest of tomorrow.
