What is MeetMyHarvest?

+

MeetMyHarvest is a platform that connects farmers and buyers, allowing buyers to purchase agricultural products in bulk directly from farmers.

How do I sign up as a farmer?

+

To sign up as a farmer, visit our registration page and follow the provided instructions. You'll need to provide some essential information about your farm and products.

Can I request a sample of a product?

+

Yes, you can request samples of products from farmers. You can contact the respective farmer and inquire about sample availability and pricing.
